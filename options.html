How to change shortcuts for closing tabs

Step 1: open this URL in Chrome: chrome://extensions/shortcuts

Look for this extension : "Keyboard shortcuts to close Chrome tabs"

Step 2: Configure the hotkeys

Notes

  • If you enable the keyboard in Global, ensure you don't have other macro or program running in background.
  • Certain tools such as Karabiner, Automator (Mac) or Autohotkey (Windows), can interfere with the extension. Ensure you don't have such programs running in the background and your hotkeys are not conflicted.
